Our Purpose

A lot of Filipinos need to find free or low-cost mental health institutions, consultations with licensed professionals, trusted medicine sources, and suicide prevention hotlines. We also need to information or more understanding about the mental health basics. We need to break the stigma regarding mental health in the Philippines.

Scenarios

  • Where can I book free or low-cost consultations for a mental health professional in the Philippines?
  • What suicide hotlines can I contact immediately for crisis support?
