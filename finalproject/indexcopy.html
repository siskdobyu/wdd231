What is mental health?

Site Purpose

• The site will serve as a central hub to help Filipinos find free or low-cost mental health institutions, consultations with licensed professionals, trusted medicine sources, and suicide prevention hotlines. It will also provide short educational descriptions of common mental health issues.

Scenarios

  • Where can I book free or low-cost consultations for a mental health professional in the Philippines?
  • What suicide hotlines can I contact immediately for crisis support?
