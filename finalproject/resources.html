Free or Low-Cost Consultations

A list of platforms and programs offering teletherapy and virtual mental health support, often at reduced rates or free on certain days.

  • LGU/MSWD Telemedicine Programs: Check your local government unit's (LGU) or Municipal Social Welfare and Development (MSWD) office for subsidized or free virtual counseling services.
  • University Psychology Clinics: Many universities (e.g., UP PsycServ (Psychosocial Services), Ateneo Bulatao Center, UST Mental Health Responders (Viber/Text)) offer free or very low-cost teletherapy sessions conducted by supervised graduate students.
  • MindCare Club: is a pioneering tele-mental healthcare service provider in the Philippines that offers online consultations with a network of psychiatrists, psychologists, and counselors. They provide comprehensive, accessible, affordable, and high-quality mental health support o Filipinos both locally and globally.
  • KonsultaMD: Offers 24/7 telemedicine, including mental health consults. Fees apply, but look for subscription options.

Medicine Access

Information on where to find affordable psychiatric medications and programs to help ease the cost of treatment.

Affordable Medication Sources

  • Medicine Access Programs: Inquire about programs like Botika ng Bayan or DOH drug assistance at local health centers.
  • Generic Pharmacies: Stores specializing in generics (e.g., Generika) often carry commonly prescribed mental health meds at lower prices than major chains.
  • Discounts: Utilize senior citizen, PWD, or PhilHealth benefits where applicable.

Important Medication Information

  • Prescription Reminders: Always consult a licensed psychiatrist for proper diagnosis and prescription. Never self-medicate.
  • Commonly Prescribed Meds: (Disclaimer: This is NOT medical advice.) Common classes include SSRIs (antidepressants) and anti-anxiety medications, but the specific drug depends entirely on a doctor's evaluation.
